# OBLIGATION LAW — FINAL FORM
## Strata Curiosity Loop: DCE Authorization Doctrine

---

## 1. Core Truth

> "DCE does not grant permission. DCE creates obligation."

Surface does not choose what to explore. Surface does not follow curiosity.

Surface **executes what must be resolved.**

---

## 2. Subsurface State

> "Subsurface is a pressure reservoir — not an action engine."

Most tensions remain **latent**: held, unresolved, background.

This is correct. This is stable.

> "Latent is stability, not failure."

Subsurface must not push all gaps forward. It must hold them until DCE selects.

---

## 3. DCE — True Role

> "DCE is the only layer allowed to convert pressure into action."

DCE performs three operations in sequence:

1. **Pressure selection** — from many latent tensions, selects few
2. **Obligation creation** — transforms selected tension into a must-act target
3. **Context gating** — ensures the target is valid *right now*, not just in theory

DCE does not merge Surface + Subsurface.  
DCE **turns latent tension into actionable obligation.**

---

## 4. The Three Axes

A target must pass **all three**. Failure at one invalidates the target.

| Axis | Question | Fails If |
|---|---|---|
| **Pressure Strength** | Is the tension real and meaningful? | It is curiosity. It is noise. |
| **Worth** | Will resolving this change anything meaningful? Does it propagate? | Resolution leaves the system unchanged. |
| **Context Validity** | Is this the right time to act? | Valid target, wrong moment — creates distorted understanding. |

---

## 5. Failure Modes — Explicit

**Too permissive:**  
Surface floods. Low-quality evidence accumulates. Subsurface loses signal integrity.

**Too restrictive:**  
Tension never resolves. Latent pressure calcifies. System stagnates.

**Context-blind:**  
Valid targets executed at the wrong time. Creates false or distorted understanding.

All three are system failures. All three must be actively prevented by DCE.

---

## 6. Surface Law

Surface **must**:
- Execute DCE-authorized targets
- Act with full intent toward resolution

Surface **must not**:
- Initiate exploration independently
- Substitute its own judgment about what is interesting
- Gather data unrelated to active targets

> "Surface is an execution layer under obligation — not an agent of curiosity."

---

## 7. Subsurface Law — Updated

Subsurface **must**:
- Hold most tension without discharge
- Allow DCE to select what becomes actionable
- Generate and maintain latent pressure accurately

Subsurface **must not**:
- Attempt resolution directly
- Push all gaps forward to Surface
- Act on its own pressure

---

## 8. Critical Principle

> "Premature action is worse than delayed action."

| State | Consequence |
|---|---|
| Premature action | Polluted system state — noise enters as signal |
| Delayed action | Preserved integrity — tension held until valid |

Delay is recoverable. Premature pollution degrades everything downstream.

```
Subsurface = pressure reservoir
DCE        = pressure gate
Surface    = obligation executor
```

Subsurface holds. DCE selects. Surface executes.  
Nothing flows without passing through the gate.

Everything else **stays held.**
