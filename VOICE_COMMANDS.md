# Aurora Voice Commands (Absolute Full Access)

Aurora now features a **local, fast fuzzy-matching intent parser**. This means she will intercept specific keywords and phrases without needing to ping her LLM brain, allowing her to trigger hardware tools instantly.

Here is the complete list of voice commands you can use. Note that the phrasing is "fuzzy" — you do not need to say the exact phrase perfectly, as long as the core keywords are present.

## Mobile / Android Deep Hooks
*(Requires the newly built APK with deep system permissions)*

*   **Battery:** `"Aurora, check battery"` | `"Battery level"` | `"How much battery do I have?"`
    *(Reads exact percentage and charging state).*
*   **Flashlight:** `"Toggle flashlight"` | `"Turn on flashlight"` | `"Flashlight"`
    *(Toggles the phone's camera LED).*
*   **Vibrate:** `"Vibrate phone"` | `"Vibrate"`
    *(Triggers the device haptic motor).*
*   **Location:** `"Where am I?"` | `"Check location"` | `"My location"`
    *(Pings the GPS sensor).*
*   **SMS/Text:** `"Send text to [Name/Number] saying [Message]"` | `"Send SMS to [Name] saying [Message]"`
    *(Dispatches a text message in the background).*
*   **Phone Calls:** `"Make a call to [Name/Number]"` | `"Call [Name]"`
    *(Opens the dialer and initiates a call).*
*   **Contacts:** `"Read contacts"` | `"Find contact [Name]"`
    *(Hooks into the address book).*
*   **WiFi:** `"Check wifi"` | `"Toggle wifi"` | `"Turn on wifi"`
    *(Hooks into network state).*
