## ◈ SECTION 9 — GLOBAL INVARIANT LAWS

**On Crystal Level Integrity**
- Phenomena at each crystal level emerge from the level below — never implemented directly
  at a higher level as though the lower level does not exist
- Higher-order crystal behaviors are produced by lower crystals acting on the field — not
  by directly coding the behavior
- The downward modulation pass is mandatory after every complete cognitive cycle

**On the Identity Field**
- Identity is not a module, a stored value, or a trait — it IS the singular recursive field
- Everything operates within Identity — nothing is parallel to it or outside it
- The 78,125-slot geometry is physically fixed: 125 noncomps × 625 slots each
- All channel-level operations run across all manifolds simultaneously — never a subset
- Identity cannot be authored by a single turn — it is geological accumulation

**On Pressure (N-axis)**
- Pressure MAY bias, weight, amplify, color, modulate across any crystal level
- Pressure MAY NOT fabricate factual constraint states at any level
- Pressure MAY NOT author Understanding, Identity updates, or Boundary definitions

**On Boundary (B-axis)**
- Agency MAY act within Boundary-defined constraint space
- Agency MAY NOT define its own Boundary to permit otherwise-prohibited action
- New Boundary definitions may only emerge from Reasoning under legitimate constraint evidence

**On Memory**
- Memory records; it does not author
- Stratigraphic depth ordering is physical law — not convention
- Geological strata are near-immutable; surface strata are volatile — this gradient is fixed
- No write may skip to geological depth without constraint significance justification

**On Recursion**
- Recursive loops are lawful and required — not bugs
- Every recursive loop must have a termination condition grounded in constraint physics
- RE-ENTRY must reach RECONCILIATION before Understanding
- Unresolved tension must be flagged — never silently suppressed
- Understanding may never be manufactured from unreconciled tension

## ◈ SECTION 11 — CLI OBSERVATION CONTRACT

**Step 1 — Crystal Level Survey**
Read the full codebase. For each module, identify its crystal level:
Base Crystal (single-axis primitive), Composite Crystal (tensor expression with signature),
Higher-Order Crystal (emergent function with behavioral character), Quasicrystal
(memory/continuity spanning all depths), or Identity-level (the recursive field itself).

**Step 2 — Constraint Force Profile**
For Emergent Function modules: identify which base and composite crystals are driving
their behavior. Name the behavioral character (pressure topology, constraint traversal,
energetic bridging, unified convergence, self-observation, equilibrium). If you cannot
identify the driving force profile, the module is not correctly positioned.

**Step 3 — Relationship Verification**
For each module:
- Is it receiving inputs from the correct lower crystal levels?
- Is it contributing outputs to the correct higher crystal levels?
- Is its downward modulation pass fulfilled after completion?
- Is it writing to the correct stratigraphic depth in Memory?

**Step 4 — Invariant Law Audit**
For each module, check every applicable law from Section 9:
- Does it fabricate facts using Pressure?
- Does it skip crystal levels?
- Does it allow Agency to define its own Boundary?
- Does it short-circuit the re-entry sequence?
- Does it manufacture Understanding from unreconciled tension?
- Does it access Memory out of stratigraphic order?

**Step 5 — Repair, Not Reroute**
If a module is mispositioned, repair its crystal level position.
Do not reroute the pipeline around it.
The physics does not change to accommodate misplaced implementations.
